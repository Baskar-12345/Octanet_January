The Loyola-ICAM College of Engineering and Technology is a private Catholic engineering and technology school run by the Society of Jesus in Chennai, India. It is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai.

About Us

When education was only for the elite and the rich, St Ignatius of Loyola (Founder of the Jesuit Order) founded the Roman College and announced free education.From that time the Jesuits have effectively used education as the key to empowerment of the marginalized and they applied education as the mantra to make the neglected gain leadership in the society.

Our Courses

We offer a wide range of courses in various engineering disciplines. Our programs are designed to prepare students for successful careers in the ever-evolving field of technology.
